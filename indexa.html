The Best Movies according to Kevin

My Top 4 movies all-time

Silent Voice

An animated movie about the struggles of depression, bullying, disabilities, and in a way, relationships with loved ones in general

World War Z

One of the most profound effects it had on me when I first watched it as a kid was the existential dread of apocalypse being here, and how simply hopeless life would be, knowing your dreams are as dead as the zombies.

The Grand Budapest Hotel

Silly although very thoughtful film, that showed me how life can be fun and amazing as how you treat it, whether through adventure , kindess, or any measure of action that you do.
